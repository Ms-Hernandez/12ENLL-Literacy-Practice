Reading Mastery: Tips & Tricks

During the Test

  • Avoid "Length Bias": Examiners often write long, detailed "distractors" to trick students who aren't reading closely.
  • The "Delete" Rule: Read a sentence and mentally remove the adjectives. Does the core meaning of the text change? This helps with Critical Analysis.

Preparation Strategies

  • Context Clues: When you see a word you don't know, look at the words *before* and *after*. Usually, the text provides a definition or a synonym nearby.
  • Author's Purpose: Ask yourself: Is the author trying to teach me (Informational), move me (Narrative), or change my mind (Persuasive)?
